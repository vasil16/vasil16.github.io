Where I've Worked

🎮 Game Developer → Senior Game Developer

Dumadu Studios, Bangalore

May 2023 – Present

When I joined Dumadu Studios, I hit the ground running—building casual and hyper-casual games that had to be fast, fun, and addictive. I quickly learned how crucial performance optimization is for mobile platforms: I trimmed load times, improved asset management, and rewrote messy logic into cleaner, modular systems. Over time, I got the chance to dig deeper into gameplay mechanics and experiment with AI logic. I built responsive HUDs, tweaked game feel until everything “clicked,” and even helped increase user retention through small but meaningful changes based on real player feedback. That hands-on experience laid the foundation for something bigger: stepping into VR. As I moved into a Senior Game Developer role, I started leading development on immersive VR applications—complete with physics-based object interactions, intuitive hand tracking, and environments that felt alive. The challenge? Getting all of this to run smoothly while still looking sharp. That meant optimizing rendering pipelines, refining asset workflows, and rethinking how users interact in 3D space. One of the highlights was designing AI systems that could predict trajectories and make smart decisions, adding a whole new layer of realism to gameplay. I also had the chance to mentor junior devs, which helped me grow as a communicator and team player. And while my core was game dev, I wore the software engineer hat often—debugging performance issues, designing reusable systems, and implementing real-time logic for interactive experiences. Working in Agile teams taught me how to ship features quickly without sacrificing quality. Looking back, my time at Dumadu Studios has been a crash course in everything I love: creative problem-solving, performance tuning, and bringing interactive worlds to life—whether on flat screens or in VR headsets.
